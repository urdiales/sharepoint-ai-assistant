How to Use the Mockup

1. Open a new file, Save the code above as mockup.py.

2. In your terminal, navigate to the folder where you saved mockup.py. Run: streamlit run mockup.py

3. Open a new tab or window, navigate to http://localhost:8501/. You should see your app. Click and type to interact.

4. Click on the “Connect” to fake SharePoint connection.

5. Click on a document from the sidebar to preview it Enter questions and “Send” to see mock chat.

6. Select documents in the sidebar to “Preview”.
